XILONEN - ENGRAVER OF NAMES

XILONEN - ENGRAVER OF NAMES

Xilonen is a 5-star Geo character in Genshin Impact, introduced in version 5.1. She serves as a Name Engraver for the Children of Echoes, bearing the Ancient Name "Baraka." As a blacksmith from the new region of Natlan, Xilonen is renowned for her exceptional craftsmanship, often found enjoying leisure time with naps in the sun but demonstrating unparalleled skill at the forge.

In combat, Xilonen wields a sword and functions primarily as a Geo support character. Her abilities include resistance reduction via her Elemental Skill and single-target healing from her Elemental Burst.

Beyond her smithing and combat prowess, Xilonen is depicted as a roller-skating DJ, bringing a modern flair to the game's fantasy setting. Her character trailer showcases her performing an original rap verse, highlighting her vibrant and energetic personality.

For players interested in optimizing Xilonen's performance, recommended builds focus on enhancing her support capabilities, emphasizing stats such as Defense and Energy Recharge to maximize her healing and resistance reduction effects.

Overall, Xilonen adds a unique blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary style to Genshin Impact's diverse roster of characters.
